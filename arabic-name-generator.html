What's the difference between translation and transliteration?

Translation converts the meaning of a word to another language. Transliteration converts the sound. When you "translate" your name to Arabic, you're actually transliterating it ‚Äì representing how it sounds using Arabic letters.

Why doesn't my Arabic name look exactly like I expected?
